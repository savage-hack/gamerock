What kind of game is Minecraft ?

Minecraft is a sandbox game, and as the name says it "mine-craft", you get items then try to make some good with it which is mining/sourcing then crafting.

Minecraft allows you to explore and blow your creativity without bounds. The game permits you to see yourself in different professions as you which, farmer, fisherman, traveller, cave explorer, builder and alot more. Interact with animals, mobs and villagers in the game and encounter endless fights with the hostile mobs in survival mode.The game has two major game modes; creative and survival.

In the creative game mode, you are free to work with unlimited resources and thus, craft as your mind can reach and perhaps perform your experiments.

In the survival mode, You have health and hunger bar, you have to play hard to get food shelter and items, mine for your ores, fish, craft weapons, trade with villagers, fight hostile mobs, explore for ship wrecks and lost treasures, but do everthing you can to survive!

The game has offline and online modes. Joins the servers and realms to play games with thousands of other players like you, but dont get carried away in the game with the mind blowing architectures. Among the things you do in this game is building a portal to hell and fighting with the most aggressive creatures in a hell terraformed dimension. The basic aim of every survival player is to defeat the ENDER DRAGON !
