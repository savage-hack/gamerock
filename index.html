Fashion trends of 2022 that made the year

Elizabeth Kirby
December 15, 2022 11:35 PM

  • COLD SHOULDER TOPS




    • The beginning of the year saw alot of interest in cold shoulder tops. Tops like these are commomly worn with Trousers, jean shorts, skirts etc Depending on the dresser, cold shoulder tops are easy to make a chic outfit. Alot of designers took advatage of the interest alot of people had in it and made some fabulous work on it. Some are designed with chains, some are tied, some are sewn in.

  • BAGGY TROUSERS


    • This fashion trend went for both male and female. Some dressing combo with baggy trousers are; baggy trousers with big shirt/cardigan/jacket, baggy trousers with crop top. Foot wear is easy to combine to this dressing

  • VOLUMINOUS SILHOUTTES


    • This is among one of the most selling fashion trend, some people even wear tighter shirts less these days and stores are stocking more of the voluminous silhouttes compared to tighter shirts. Voluminous silhouttes can sometimes be worn as a dress by girls, usually shorter girls. To make an outfit out of this is pretty easy. Perhaps why we buy more of the voluminous silhottes these days is because after the covid lockdown we wanted to feel free....LOL.

  • MIDI SKIRTS



    • Midi skirts were most commonly used in corporate dressings by office workers till off late when it hit the stores in varieties. The two main types of midi skirts are Pencil midi skirts commonly used in office dressings although recently alot of fashionistas integrated this in many other outfits. The other type is skater midi skirts usually used in summer outfits, dating/romantic outfits and other non-offical dressings.

  • BRA TOPS



    • Bra tops are bra-like-tops, they look like bra but aren't and are worn as tops. The way bra tops trended in 2022, tells a high tendency that we are still gonna get alot from our fashionstas with bra tops in the coming year. Bra tops can be worn with skirt, trousers and shorts.

  • SEQUIN PANTS


    • Also called "wide leg pants". This Y2K fashion hit the 2022 fashion market en masse. I very much think this will still be on our fashionistas in 2023. Sequin pants are very easy to dress with plus the variety and designs in which they come in from high waist jeans to suit pants among others.

  • CUT-OUTS


    • As the name implies, cutouts are made on clothes by cutting out fabrics from the clothe in a desired way. Alot of dress can have cutouts and good cutouts clothes are very attractive.

  • SHEER SKIRTS


    • A sheer skirt is a skirt that is semi-transparent. They could be made with; Chiffon, Goergette and gauze. Sheer skirt trended most among all skirts in 2022, infact there is still going to be alot of them in the comimg year. Sheer skirts can be used in dresses and gowns, It is in line with the Y2K fashion.

  • BIGSHIRTS AS DRESS


    • This trend is easy to make a chic outfit from, its one reason it still trends. I for one go to my bf house to try his cool shirts....LOL. This trend can be with T-shirts, packet shirts, cardighans, tops. Footwears are easily integrated into this.

  • HIGH-WAISTED TROUSERS

    • High-waist trousers became so popular in 2022, up to 60% of female trousers in major stores are high waist. High waist are likely to be plentiful is 2023 as there is no seeming substitute and it is multipurpose for wearing. Highwaist trousers can be worn to parties, work, dating, winter outfit, summer outfit, infact almost all if not all outfits.
